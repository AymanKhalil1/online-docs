Contact Filler

In some projects implicit steel sections with filler material are used to speed up the analysis process while maintaining the needed accuracy.
The video shows you how to define filler elements’ contact material in ELS’s version 9 in comparison to version 8.

To make use of ELS's Contact Filler feature follow the following procedures:

Step1:Draw a simple cantilever composed of a rectangular column and a girder made of implicit steel sections and filler material.
           This cantilever is intended to fail, fall and hit the ground.
Step2:Create a dynamic stage.
Step3:Establish a demolish scenario to remove the connection between the column & the beam.
Step4:From the Analysis menu choose Contact Options and choose the desired contact material.
Step5:Choose a bearing material undergoing weakening to compare it with version 8’s automatically set contact material.
Step6:Run the model
Step7:Keep the animation running and stop the animation at the frame where the falling cantilever beam reaches the ground.
Step8:Show the contacts.
Step9:Select the contacts with the ground.
Step10:From Tools choose Selected Contacts Details and observe the resultant force value being much less than that of version 8's.
