Edit Material Properties

Sometimes, a change to the material properties is required to be made to all the defined materials all at once.
& it's of course terribly time consuming to be changing each material one by one & for that, ELS has got your back! In ELS's version 9, you can check or change the common material properties of one material family with just one click.

For example,in a model where the concrete grade that is assigned to the columns is different from those assigned to the girders & so on so forth.
To change- for instnace- the friction coefficient of all the concrete materials in the project, follow the instructions hereunder:

Step1 From the materials browser double click on the name on the top of the hirarchy of concrete.
Step2 Choose the *Edit Material Properties* tab.
          (To select all the materials at once, check the select box.You'll find all the general common properties underneath.
          You'll find that some of them will be blank. Blank means that not all the materials share the same value of this property.)
Step4 Change - for instance- the friction coefficient to be 0.9 for all the material family.
Apply & now all the concrete materials found in this project will have a friction coefficient of 0.9.
