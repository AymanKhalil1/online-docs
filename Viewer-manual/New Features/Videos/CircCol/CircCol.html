Circular Columns

What if you want to model a circular hollow shaft or column with an outer RFT cover different from the inner one?
To account for this variation, ELS has made some changes to its circular column definition.

To make use of ELS's new modifications in the circular column feature follow the following procedures:

Step1 Open ELS & draw a circular column.
Step2 From the Styles (browser),choose circular column & circular column style1.
Step3 From the column geometry, choose in the mesh generation type : Polar.
Step4 Enter a value for the inner radius, and another for the outer radius.
Step5 From the Circular RFT, enter the external & internal covers value.
